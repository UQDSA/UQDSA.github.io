Australian Dental Association Queensland (ADAQ)

UQDSA is proudly supported by our Professional Association Supporter ADAQ. ADAQ, alongside its endorsed professional indemnity insurer, QBE, is committed to the profession of Dentistry by supporting dental students throughout their education and nurturing them in their transition from student to practitioner.

Their involvement ensures all UQ dental students are kept up to date with the latest professional information though their provision of free student membership, an initial period of free full membership and professional indemnity insurance upon graduation, and substantially discounted membership and insurance thereafter. ADAQ has been a proud supporter of UQDSA for many years and looks forward to strengthening the relationship into the future.

  http://www.adaq.com.au/

Medical Indemnity Protection Society (MIPS)

Membership of MIPS is available to health practitioners and students. Membership includes medical indemnity cover and other member benefits such as our 24-hour Clinico-Legal Support service.

MIPS protects, supports and safeguards the professional character and interests of health care practitioners and students in Australia.

Complete an application for student membership

  http://www.mips.com.au/

Dental Protection Limited (DPL)

Dental Protection Limited (DPL) is a risk management organisation providing you access to a dental indemnity policy.

DPL offers the complete package for students with a range of benefits included in free student membership. These benefits are advice and support during your course, the biannual publication Dental Student Australia, discount offers, indemnity for your elective and a range of competitions just to name a few.
